The "VIRANI GROUP" of Goa was founded by Mr. Ismail Jusab Virani of Mapusa, Goa in 1952. The group is in trading activities having experience of over sixty years, since Portuguese regime as wholesalers and retailers in various fields such as hosiery, crockery, cutlery, households, glass wares, confectionary, and later on in Payel chappals, electronic items and with all types of Indian Made Foreign liquor.

Mr. Ismail Jusab Virani, is a reputed businessman and has dedicated himself to serve the Goan Society, through his earnest social work and philanthropic contributions. He was President of Lions Club of Mapusa. He was Hon'ble Secretary of His Highness Aga Khan Shia Imami Ismaili Council for Goa and a member of Arbitration and Concillation board. He was Chairman of Indian Red Cross Society, Bardez Branch. He was also the Chairman of LokVishwas Pratishthan unanimously for 9 years, and currently he is life member of LokVishwas Pratishthan. He was the Vice Chairman for Senior Citizen Forum India, Goa Chapter and presently he is the founder member of the Forum.

His son Mahebub Virani, after successfully spreading the business of his father, has recently started his new venture "STERLING DEVELOPERS", which has launched its flagship Project "STERLING RESIDENCY", located at Calangute, Goa.
